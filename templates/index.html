The Story

A testament to the power of resilience and determination, my personal journey is one defined by grit, growth, and an unyielding thirst for knowledge. As a college dropout turned business owner, I launched a venture at 21 with a humble $10,000. Within five years, the business expanded to three locations and was generating approximately $50,000 in monthly gross sales.


During my 12-year tenure managing the company, the competitive business environment compelled me to delve into digital marketing and web development. As a phoenix rises from its ashes, I reinvented myself in the face of life changes and took a step into the mortgage industry. 


With an intent to revolutionize my work, I self-taught Python and database development to create my own CRM and outreach automation. As my applications started gaining attention, I saw an opportunity to bring this innovation to a broader audience as a freelance developer. With a repertoire of lead generation applications and automation tools created for mortgage companies and real estate brokerages, I carved a niche for myself in the industries.


Despite the COVID-19 induced setbacks within the real estate sector and surviving multiple rounds of layoffs as a loan officer, I chose to rise above the circumstances. From adversity came a revitalized focus on refining and expanding my developer skills. This led to my formal education into technology through Hardvard's CS50 course where I acquired a structured approach to software development. I further expanded my Python skills through Free Code Camp's "Data Analysis with Python".

 
Now, as a freelance software developer specializing in the real estate industry, I've honed proficiency in diverse technologies. My toolbelt includes OpenAI API, MySQL, Python, Flask, Django, Flask-MySQLdb, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Streamlit, Langchain, and Tkinter.


What fuels my incessant quest for growth is the prospect of being a better version of myself each day, demonstrating to my children that setbacks are, in fact, setups for comebacks, and that challenges in life must not deter one's spirit or dim one's ambitions. I eagerly embrace the opportunity to learn, innovate, and make a positive difference through my work.
